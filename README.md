# 📊 Superstore Sales & Profitability Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and product trends using the Superstore dataset.

---

## 🎯 Project Overview

This project provides business insights through interactive dashboards that help stakeholders monitor sales, profits, customer performance, regional contributions, and product categories.

The dashboard enables data-driven decision-making through KPI tracking and visual analytics.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 📈 Performance Overview Dashboard

* Total Sales Analysis
* Total Orders Tracking
* Total Quantity Sold
* Average Discount Monitoring
* Sales by Region
* Sales by Category
* Top Customers Analysis
* Monthly Sales Trends

### 💰 Profitability Insights Dashboard

* Total Profit Analysis
* Profit Margin Tracking
* Average Profit Per Order
* Total Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)
* Profit by Customer Segment
* Monthly Quantity Analysis
* Profit Margin Trends
* Profit by Product Sub-Category

### 🎛 Interactive Filters

* Category Filter

  * Furniture
  * Office Supplies
  * Technology

* Region Filter

  * Central
  * East
  * South
  * West

* Segment Filter

  * Consumer
  * Corporate
  * Home Office

## 📈 Business Insights

### Sales Analysis

* Technology generated the highest sales.
* West region contributed the largest share of revenue.
* Monthly sales increased significantly during the final quarter.
* A few customers accounted for a substantial portion of total sales.

### Profitability Analysis

* Consumer segment generated the highest profit.
* Copiers were the most profitable sub-category.
* Profit margins remained consistently positive throughout the year.
* Quantity sold peaked during the final months of the year.
